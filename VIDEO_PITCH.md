# 🎥 OpenMind Nexus: 3-Minute Video Pitch Script

This script is optimized for a 3-minute screen-recorded video pitch. It tells the story of "Cognitive Risk," demonstrates the Band multi-agent orchestration, and proves nuance.

**Target Length:** 3:00  
**Pacing:** Confident, enterprise-focused, steady.

---

## 🎬 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook & The Problem
**VISUAL:** 
- Title Card: "OpenMind Nexus: Multi-Agent Cognitive Risk Intelligence"
- Cut to a split screen showing a traditional firewall on the left, and a fake "Urgent Expose!" polarizing social media post on the right.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Enterprises spend billions defending their networks from malware. But today, the most dangerous attacks bypass firewalls entirely and target human psychology. 
> 
> We call these Information Integrity Incidents—highly targeted disinformation, deepfakes, and manipulative media designed to exploit cognitive biases. Identifying them takes hours of manual analyst review. 
> 
> Today, we're solving that. Meet **OpenMind Nexus**: a multi-agent cognitive risk investigation platform powered by Band orchestration."

---

## 💻 0:30 - 1:30 | The Hero Demo (High Risk)
**VISUAL:** 
- Screen recording of the **Orchestration Dashboard**.
- Presenter copies **Incident 1 (High Risk)** from `DEMO_INCIDENTS.md` and pastes it into the input. 
- Click "Start Multi-Agent Analysis". 
- Zoom in on the "Live Band Room" as the agents start popping up with JSON payloads.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Let's look at a live Information Integrity Incident. When suspicious content is flagged, our Content Intake Agent securely posts the metadata to a shared Band context room.
> 
> Immediately, four specialized agents get to work. 
> 
> The **Cognitive Bias Agent** scans for psychological manipulation, detecting 'Emotional Reasoning'. 
> At the same time, the **Verification Agent** flags 'urgency manipulation'. 
> The **Echo Chamber Agent** calculates the network amplification risk as HIGH.
> 
> Finally, the **Explainability Agent** aggregates this shared context from the Band room and synthesizes a human-readable reasoning trace."

**VISUAL:**
- Mouse pans over to the **Human Oversight Panel** on the right side of the screen, highlighting the red ESCALATE recommendation.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Notice that no single agent made the escalation decision. Escalation is an emergent outcome of multiple independent evidence streams. The AI doesn't make the final call—it empowers the human analyst with a transparent audit trail to escalate to Trust & Safety."

---

## ⚖️ 1:30 - 2:00 | Proving Nuance (Medium/Low Risk)
**VISUAL:** 
- Quick montage. Paste **Incident 2 (Medium Risk)**, fast-forward through the Band Room, highlight the yellow **MONITOR** recommendation.
- Paste **Incident 3 (Low Risk)**, fast-forward, highlight the green **ARCHIVE** recommendation.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "But a security tool that flags everything is useless. Our multi-agent workflow is highly nuanced. 
> 
> When analyzing vague 'experts agree' claims, the agents output a 'MONITOR' recommendation for the gray area. And for neutral financial reports, the system correctly identifies it as safe, choosing to 'ARCHIVE' it rather than wasting analyst time."

---

## 🏗️ 2:00 - 2:30 | Architecture & "Why Band?"
**VISUAL:** 
- Display the Transport Layer diagram from `ARCHITECTURE.md` showing Development Mode vs Production Mode.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "Why use Band for this? Because an investigation isn't a single API call. Each agent contributes independent evidence, maintains state, and passes structured context. 
> 
> Band provides the collaborative transport layer where these artifacts and decisions become auditable. Our architecture is designed Band-first. The orchestrator you just saw contains a feature-flagged integration interface, allowing us to instantly transition from our local compatibility layer to native Band Rooms in production."

---

## 🚀 2:30 - 3:00 | The Vision & Closing
**VISUAL:** 
- Quick shots of the legacy UI (the Feed Sliders and the Insights Chart) to show the depth of the tech.
- Final Title Card with Team Name and Contact Info.

**VOICEOVER:**
> "OpenMind Nexus is built on top of our proprietary bias analysis and influence-visualization engine. 
> 
> We are transforming how organizations investigate, trace, and respond to information integrity risks. By separating reasoning from transport, we've built a verifiable, multi-agent enterprise workflow.
> 
> Thank you."

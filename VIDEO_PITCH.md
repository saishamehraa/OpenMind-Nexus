# 🎥 OpenMind Nexus: 3-Minute Video Pitch Script

## 🎬 0:00 - 0:30 | The Hook & The Problem

**VISUAL:**

* Title Card: "OpenMind Nexus: Multi-Agent Cognitive Risk Intelligence"
* Split screen: traditional firewall on the left, manipulative social media content on the right.

**VOICEOVER:**

"Enterprises spend billions defending their networks from malware. But today, some of the most damaging attacks bypass firewalls entirely and target human psychology.

Organizations are increasingly exposed to information integrity incidents—highly targeted disinformation, deepfakes, and manipulative content designed to influence human decision-making. Identifying these threats often requires hours of manual review.

Today, we're solving that. Meet OpenMind Nexus: a multi-agent cognitive risk investigation platform powered by Band orchestration."

---

## 💻 0:30 - 1:30 | The Hero Demo (High Risk)

**VISUAL:**

* Open Orchestration Dashboard.
* Paste High-Risk Incident.
* Click "Start Multi-Agent Analysis".
* Fast-forward until ESCALATE appears.
* Zoom into Live Band Room.

**VOICEOVER:**

"Let's look at a live incident.

When suspicious content is submitted, our Content Intake Agent publishes structured metadata into a shared Band investigation room.

Within seconds, the system recommends ESCALATION. Why? Let's inspect the evidence trail.

Four specialized agents collaborated here.

The Cognitive Bias Agent scanned for psychological manipulation.

The Verification Agent assessed source credibility and identified unsupported claims.

The Echo Chamber Agent calculated amplification and polarization risk.

The Explainability Agent aggregated this shared context and generated a transparent reasoning trace.

Each agent contributed a unique evidence artifact into the shared investigation room."

**VISUAL:**

* Highlight individual agent outputs.
* Return to ESCALATE recommendation.
* Pause 2–3 seconds.

**VOICEOVER:**

"Notice that no single agent made the escalation decision.

Escalation is an emergent outcome of multiple independent evidence streams.

The AI doesn't make the final call—it equips human analysts with an auditable trail of evidence to support Trust and Safety decisions."

---

## ⚖️ 1:30 - 2:00 | Proving Nuance

**VISUAL:**

* Medium-Risk Incident → MONITOR.
* Low-Risk Incident → ARCHIVE.

**VOICEOVER:**

"But a security platform that flags everything is not useful.

Our workflow is designed to distinguish between high-risk, ambiguous, and low-risk content.

For uncertain claims, the agents recommend MONITOR, preserving human oversight.

For a neutral financial report, the Cognitive Bias Agent identifies Balanced Thinking rather than manipulation. Combined with strong credibility and low polarization risk, the Explainability Agent recommends ARCHIVE.

This dramatically reduces false positives and allows analysts to focus on the incidents that matter most."

---

## 🏗️ 2:00 - 2:30 | Architecture & Why Band

**VISUAL:**

* Transport Layer diagram.

**VOICEOVER:**

"Without Band, these agents become tightly coupled API calls.

With Band, each agent contributes independent evidence, shares structured context, and participates in an auditable investigation workflow.

We are separating reasoning from transport.

The orchestrator you just saw includes a feature-flagged integration layer, allowing us to move seamlessly from our local compatibility environment to native Band Rooms in production."

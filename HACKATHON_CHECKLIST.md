## 3. Q&A Preparation ❓
- [ ] **Why Band?** -> "Because the investigation is not a single transaction. Each agent contributes independent evidence, maintains state, and hands structured context to downstream agents. Band gives us a collaboration layer where those artifacts, decisions, and escalations become auditable and shareable across humans and AI systems. We are separating reasoning from transport."
- [ ] **How is this different from ChatGPT?** -> "It's an automated, orchestrated pipeline of specialized agents designed for enterprise triage, not a single chatbot."
- [ ] **What's the business model?** -> "B2B SaaS for enterprise security, trust & safety teams, and intelligence firms."

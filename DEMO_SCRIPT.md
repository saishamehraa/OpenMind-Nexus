## ❓ Anticipated Questions & Answers

### Q: "Why use Band instead of just calling the APIs sequentially?"
**A:** "Band provides a robust, stateful shared context. It allows agents to subscribe to updates asynchronously, prevents context window bloat by structuring the data, and gives us an audit trail of how the agents reached their conclusion—crucial for enterprise compliance."

### Q: "How accurate is the bias detection?"
**A:** "Because we use specialized, modular agents, we can fine-tune the Cognitive Bias Agent specifically for psychological manipulation, rather than relying on a generic LLM prompt. The accuracy is much higher when the agent's sole purpose is bias detection."

### Q: "Who is the target customer?"
**A:** "Enterprise security teams, trust and safety departments at media companies, and intelligence analysts who need to triage massive amounts of potentially manipulative information."
